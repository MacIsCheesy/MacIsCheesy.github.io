ART APPRECIATION ESSAY

Christianity's Perspective On Art

Made by: Ignasius Alvin Yo, Kevin Reinaldi, Sherlyn Chang (5A5)

Due: 27 November 2014

&emsp As we know, art originally comes from human and it is a synonym of knowledge. In
this modern period, we can see art of the essence of expression and human creativity. Art is
very hard to be explained and also to be rated. Because each artist chooses the rule and
parameters which leads their work by itself. Art is the expression or application of human
creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture,
producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty of emotional power. Art begins
when one person, with the object of joining another or others to himself in one and the same
feeling, expresses that feeling by certain external indications. The are various feelings of the
artists that may infect others like feelings love one's own country, self devotion and
submission to fate or to God expressed in a drama, and many more.
